Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Summary: We collect minimal data necessary to provide our software tools. Uploaded credit reports are processed in-memory and are not stored on our servers. We do not sell your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly:

  • Account Information: Email address and password when you create an account
  • Uploaded Documents: Credit reports you upload for analysis (processed in-memory, not stored)
  • Generated Documents: Dispute letters and correspondence you create using our tools
  • Usage Data: Actions taken within the platform for audit trail purposes

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide and maintain our software tools
  • Generate dispute documentation based on your inputs
  • Maintain audit trails for your records
  • Send essential service communications
  • Improve our software functionality

3. Credit Report Processing

Credit reports you upload are processed in-memory for analysis purposes. The raw report files are not stored on our servers. Extracted data (tradelines, account information) is stored only in your account for your ongoing use and can be deleted at your request.

4. Data Retention

We retain your data as follows:

  • Account data: Retained while your account is active
  • Audit logs: Retained for your records and exportable at any time
  • Generated documents: Retained until you delete them

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only:

  • With service providers who assist in operating our platform (under strict confidentiality)
  • When required by law or valid legal process
  • To protect our rights or the safety of users

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit and at rest, access controls, and regular security assessments. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

8. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at: privacy@605b.ai

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated via email or prominent notice on our platform.
