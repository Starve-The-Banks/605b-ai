## SECTION 3 — ROTATION PLAN

### Phase 1: Initial Launch (First 7 Days)
1. **Script 1 "Hidden Errors"** — Broad cold traffic, highest budget
2. **Script 2 "Identity Theft Recovery"** — Problem-aware lookalikes
3. **Script 3 "Collections Confusion"** — Problem-aware lookalikes

**Audience Strategy:**
- **Broad Cold Traffic:** Script 1 only
- **Problem-Aware Users:** Scripts 2 & 3 split test
- **Identity-Theft Aware:** Script 2 focused

### Phase 2: Optimization (Days 8-21)
- Continue best performers from Phase 1
- Add Scripts 4 & 5 as variants
- Test competitor-aware audiences with Script 4

### Target Segments by Script:
- **Scripts 1, 3, 5:** General credit-conscious users
- **Script 2:** Identity theft victims, fraud-aware users
- **Script 4:** Credit repair service searchers, competitor audiences

## SECTION 4 — HEYGEN PRODUCTION NOTES

### Avatar Selection
- **Style:** Professional financial advisor appearance
- **Age Range:** 30-45 (trustworthy authority figure)
- **Attire:** Business casual, navy or charcoal
- **Ethnicity:** Rotate across demos for A/B testing

### Voice and Delivery
- **Tone:** Confident but not aggressive
- **Pace:** Measured, clear enunciation
- **Emphasis:** Strong opening hook, confident closing
- **Style:** "Knowledgeable peer" not "salesperson"

### Production Specs
- **Background:** Clean, professional (soft office/home office)
- **Lighting:** Bright, even (professional but not corporate)
- **Captions:** Always enabled, white text, dark stroke
- **Logo Integration:** Fade-in at 0s, persistent watermark, center-close at end

### Format Specifications
- **Facebook Feed:** 1:1 Square, 4:5 Vertical
- **Instagram Feed:** 1:1 Square, 4:5 Vertical  
- **Instagram Reels:** 9:16 Vertical
- **Instagram Stories:** 9:16 Vertical

### Technical Requirements
- **Resolution:** 1080p minimum
- **Frame Rate:** 30fps
- **Audio:** Clear, noise-free, proper levels
- **File Size:** <50MB for optimal Meta delivery

### Logo Animation Sequence
1. **0-2s:** Logo fade-in bottom-right with hook
2. **2-16s:** Subtle watermark (favicon.svg) 15% opacity
3. **16-22s:** Center logo (605b-bracket-box.svg) with CTA text
4. **Final frame:** Logo + "605b.ai" hold for 2s
