## BANNED PHRASES — DO NOT USE ANYWHERE

### Outcome Promises
- "Fix your credit"
- "Repair your credit"
- "Boost your score" / "Raise your score" / "Increase your credit score"
- "Guaranteed results" / "Guaranteed deletion" / "Guaranteed removal"
- "Remove negative items" / "Delete collections" / "Clean your credit"
- "We will remove…" / "We fix…"
- "Improve your credit rating"
- "Get items deleted"

### Service Language (implies acting on behalf)
- "We dispute on your behalf"
- "We contact bureaus for you"
- "We handle your disputes"
- "Our team will…" / "Let us take care of…"
- "We send letters for you"
- "We work with creditors"

### Before/After Claims
- Any before/after credit score imagery
- "Before: 520 → After: 740"
- "See how much your score improved"
- Any numeric score change claims
- Screenshots showing score improvements

### Personal Attribute Violations (Meta policy)
- "Is your credit score low?"
- "Struggling with bad credit?"
- "Tired of being denied?"
- "Are you a victim of identity theft?"
- Any second-person question implying personal financial hardship

### Urgency / Scarcity
- "Limited time offer"
- "Act now before it's too late"
- "Don't wait" / "Hurry"
- "Only X spots left"
- "This won't last"

### Competitor Disparagement
- "Stop paying monthly for credit repair"
- "Credit repair companies overcharge"
- Direct competitor name comparisons
- "Unlike [competitor]…"

### Subscription Language
- "Subscribe" / "Monthly plan" / "Recurring"
- "Cancel anytime" (implies subscription exists)
- "Free trial" (implies conversion to subscription)
