## Do Not Overbuild: MVP Trust Layer

The goal of social presence at launch is **trust and discoverability**, not engagement optimization.

**Do:**
- Fill out all profile fields completely
- Post the compliance thread / disclaimers early
- Maintain consistent branding (name, logo, colors)
- Link back to 605b.ai from all profiles

**Do not:**
- Spend time on content calendars beyond week 1
- Set up complex bot automations before you have users
- Create more than the minimum Discord channels
- Post more than 1x/day on LinkedIn
- Buy followers or engagement
- Engage in credit repair community drama

**Social accounts exist to:**
1. Prove the company is real (Meta reviewer signal)
2. Provide a support channel
3. Build organic discoverability over time

Everything else is premature optimization.
