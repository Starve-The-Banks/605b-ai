# Meta Ads — Policy-Safe Launch Stack

**Brand:** 605b.ai
**Parent Company:** Ninth Wave Analytics LLC
**Product Type:** Self-service credit analysis and identity theft recovery software
**Billing Model:** One-time in-app purchase only (non-consumable)

---

## SPECIAL AD CATEGORY — MANDATORY

**All 605b.ai campaigns MUST be created under the "Credit" Special Ad Category.**

### Why

Meta's "Credit" Special Ad Category applies broadly to ads relating to credit opportunities. While 605b.ai is software (not a credit product), the subject matter — credit reports, dispute letters, credit bureaus, FCRA — will trigger automated review. Proactive classification prevents account suspension.

### Impact on Targeting

| Targeting Feature | Available? |
|---|---|
| Age targeting | No |
| Gender targeting | No |
| ZIP / postal code targeting | No |
| Lookalike audiences | No |
| Interest-based targeting | Limited |
| Broad / contextual | Yes |
| Custom audiences (site visitors) | Yes (with restrictions) |

The targeting loss is recoverable through content strategy. Account suspension is not recoverable.

---

## BANNED PHRASES — DO NOT SAY

The following phrases and patterns must NEVER appear in any Meta ad creative, landing page linked from ads, or ad-adjacent content:

### Outcome Promises
- "Fix your credit"
- "Repair your credit"
- "Boost your score"
- "Increase your credit score"
- "Raise your score by X points"
- "Guaranteed results"
- "Guaranteed deletion"
- "Guaranteed removal"
- "Remove negative items"
- "Delete collections"
- "Clean your credit"
- "We will remove…"
- "We fix…"

### Service Language (implies acting on behalf)
- "We dispute on your behalf"
- "We contact bureaus for you"
- "We handle your disputes"
- "Our team will…"
- "Let us take care of…"
- "We send letters for you"

### Before/After Claims
- Any before/after credit score imagery
- "Before 605b.ai: 520 → After: 740"
- "See how much your score improved"
- Any numeric score change claims

### Personal Attribute Violations
- "Is your credit score low?"
- "Struggling with bad credit?"
- "Tired of being denied?"
- "Are you a victim of identity theft?"
- Any second-person question implying personal financial attributes

### Competitor Disparagement
- "Stop paying monthly for credit repair"
- "Credit repair companies overcharge"
- Direct competitor name comparisons
- Industry-bashing language

## WARM-UP PLAN — 4 WEEKS

### Week 1 (Days 1-7): Foundation
- **Objective:** Traffic / Landing Page Views
- **Daily Budget:** $5–10
- **Audience:** Broad interest targeting (within Special Ad Category constraints)
- **Creative:** Identity theft education angle. Link to homepage or about page.
- **Goal:** Establish spend history. Zero conversion expectation.
- **Rule:** Do NOT optimize for conversions in week 1.

### Week 2 (Days 8-14): Expand
- **Objective:** Traffic / Landing Page Views
- **Daily Budget:** $15–25
- **Creative:** A/B test identity theft angle vs. credit documentation angle
- **Action:** If any ad is rejected, do NOT resubmit immediately. Adjust copy, wait 24h, then resubmit.
- **Rule:** Still traffic objective only. Do NOT switch to conversions.

### Week 3 (Days 15-21): Optimize
- **Objective:** ViewContent optimization
- **Daily Budget:** $25–50
- **Creative:** Best performers from week 2 + new variants
- **Pixel Events:** Optimize for ViewContent (pricing page views)
- **Retargeting:** Create 7-day site visitor audience. Do NOT run retargeting ads yet.

### Week 4 (Days 22-28): Convert
- **Objective:** Conversions (InitiateCheckout or Purchase)
- **Daily Budget:** $50–75
- **Creative:** Winning creatives + retargeting-specific variants
- **Audiences:** Prospecting + retargeting (7-day visitors)
- **Scale Rule:** Increase budget max 20-30% every 3-5 days.

### Ongoing (Day 29+)
- **Daily Budget:** $75–150+ based on ROAS
- **Refresh creative** every 2 weeks
- **Monitor frequency** — if >2.5, rotate creative
- **Budget increases:** Never >30% in a single day

## BUDGET RAMP — STRICT RULES

| Day | Max Daily Budget | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| 1-3 | $5 | Single ad set, single creative |
| 4-7 | $10 | Add second creative |
| 8-10 | $15 | Evaluate CTR. If <1%, revise copy. |
| 11-14 | $25 | Best performing creative only |
| 15-17 | $30 | Switch to ViewContent optimization |
| 18-21 | $50 | Add retargeting audience |
| 22-28 | $75 | Switch to conversion optimization |
| 29+ | Scale 20-30% per 3-5 days | Cap at sustainable ROAS |

**Hard rule:** Never increase daily budget more than 30% in a single day. Meta's algorithm destabilizes with sudden jumps, which can also trigger account review.
